Wind Speed

Wind Speed VS Latitude, this figure is the result of scatter plotting the cleaned date frame for wind speed that were recorded over the world on 10/01/2022, the city latitude coordinate being used as x axis while the city wind speed is used as y axis.
the results shows that:

  • almost a symmetric distribution, Latitude has not much impact on wind speed at the time that the data have been collected, on average wind speed at the Northern hemisphere looks little bit faster than the wind speed on southern hemisphereand, we can see more cities results higher the latitude 40 as the northern hemisphere has more cities data than the Southern hemisphere cities
